Where to Stay

Footprints is a boutique resort with unique, cozy spaces. Accommodation options range from traditional rustic log cabins to modern lakeside rooms.

Rustic Experience

Traditional Log Cabins

Classic, free-standing lakeside cabins tucked into the trees. Features comfortable beds, essential amenities, and a front porch to take in the crisp morning air.

Cozy Setup

Resort Rooms

Convenient hotel-style rooms located closer to the main lodge and common spaces. Ideal for individuals or couples looking for a clean, simple home base.

Glamping & Camping Sites
